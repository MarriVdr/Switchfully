Images

This page covers image formats, but you may also be looking for links and useful extras.

JPGs

JPG images are good for photos.

GIFs

GIFs are good for animation.

PNG Images

PNGs are good for diagrams and icons.

SVG Images

SVGs are amazing. Use them wherever you can.

But, for SVGs to display consistently across browsers, one need to convert text fields to outlines (image editor).
